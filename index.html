Hello! I'm Roni and I'm a Washington, DC-based Distinguished Engineer of Data Science @ Capital One. Previous to that, I've been a fractional CTO, Head of Data Science @ DrFirst, and a machine learning engineer / data scientist / statistician / data person in the tech industry. Before working the in the tech industry, I was a PhD environmental epidemiologist carrying out research @ the Icahn School of Medicine @ Mount Sinai in New York City.

I'm the creator and maintainer of the web service Data Compass AI, a free web service to make the data maturity levels of various organizations transparent.

I'm also the creator and maintainer of the open-source python package causal-curve, and I also maintain this ridiculously long list of resources for data leaders.

I've also spoken at multiple data science conferences, primarily on the topic of causality and data.

What I do and love:

  • helping orgs become more data-driven and establishing excellent processes/patterns
  • building teams and mentoring (and learning from) junior and senior data analysts, scientists, and engineers
  • architecting data and machine learning platforms
  • keeping up to date with the constantly evolving modern data-stack
  • helping build out an organization's data strategy

I love meeting people so please don't hesitate to reach out!
